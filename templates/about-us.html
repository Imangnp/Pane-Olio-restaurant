The Restaurant

Pane e Olio is an authentic Italian restaurant located in the heart of Stockholm. Our mission is to provide our guests with a true taste of Italy through fresh, authentic cuisine and carefully selected wines. Our chefs use only the finest, locally sourced ingredients to create delicious dishes that are bursting with flavor. We believe that good food should be accompanied by good wine, which is why we have an extensive wine list featuring a variety of Italian wines to complement our menu at Pane e Olio, we pride ourselves on providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere where our guests can enjoy a relaxing and memorable dining experience.

Head Chef's Menu

Whether he's preparing a classic pasta dish or reinventing a traditional recipe, Mario's commitment to quality and innovation is always on display.
